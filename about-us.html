About Us

Nairagram is a digital financial service company dedicated to the African community. With Nairagram, your money transfer is instantly remitted to your beneficiary.

With our in -depth knowledge of the Naija and African cultures, habits, interests, and needs, our goal is to make sending money back home to “mama”, “Papa”, “Sista”, “Brotha”, or even “Padi” as seamless as possible from the comfort of your mobile device. Download our app and you can enjoy money transfer the Naija way! It’s Faster, Cheaper, and easy to use.
